Hermitcraft Season 8 Economy

Introduction

This Project is made in colloboration with these trackers, who each recorded the various transactions of Hermitcraft members. We were only interested in transactions that were made with diamonds. All episodes from each hermit were checked and we incorporated as many livestreams as we could.

This chord diagram represents the flow of diamonds between hermits.

You can hover over a hermit's name to see all transactions in and out or hover over an individual ribbon to see how many diamonds transferred between 2 specific hermits during Season 8.

Selecting a hermit in the dropdown will highlight transactions between them and other hermits without having to hover.
This Sankey diagram is another way of representing the flow of diamonds between hermits.

You can find your favourite hermit and trace the lines from the Payers (The ones who spend the diamonds) to the Payee (The ones who receive the diamonds.) This way you can easily track how many diamonds were traded between 2 hermits over the whole season indicated by the thickness of the ribbon connecitng them, as well as a really good indication of how much a hermit spent and received in total over the season indicated by the height of their bars on the left and right.
